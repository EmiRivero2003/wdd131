WDD131 - Project

GamerZone

Name: GamerZone

Description: I choose that name because I want to create a page that each gamer can buy his favortie game and the best periferics that he need to have fun at the best price. The name is clear and perfectly communicates what each gamer is looking for.

Domain: GamerZone.com

Site Purpouse

The objetive for this is simple and clear, is only for gamers, in this site they can find games of all geners, shooters, race, adventure, simulation, RPG, etc. Each player need something for play his favorite games, that`s why in GamerZone we not only sell games, we sell periferics, each player can find, keyboards, mouse, joysticks, headphones, etc.

Scenarios

  • What mouse is better to play tactical shooter games?
  • What weel is better to play simulation games?
  • What are the most popular games?
  • Do you have mechanical keyboards?

Color Schema

Chosen colors

  • Primary: #121212 - Dark Gray/Black (for backgrounds)
  • Secondary: #0FA6A0 - Teal (for buttons, links, highlights)
  • Accent: #4B1281 - Intense Violet (for headings, hover effects, gamer highlights)
  • Text: #FFFFFF - White (for body text)

    • Typography

    Selected Fonts:

    • Orbitron - Used for headings and titles (futuristic/gamer look).
    • Roboto - Used for body text and paragraphs (clean and readable).
